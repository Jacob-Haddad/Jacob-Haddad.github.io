What is AI?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is defined by Webster as "The capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior". Think of a computer being able to think, act, and make decisions without human interaction. Sounds cool right? Healthcare and Medical Advantages

The application of AI in everyday life is endless, but one of the biggest places people point to when talking about the usefulness of AI is the medical field. Artificial Intelligence could be used to store, organize and retrieve the information on patients and their conditions. In medical diagnosis we can create programs that simulate expert human reasoning. Artificial intelligence could make the work of doctors easier and faster as electronic health records are used. Whether the benefits of AI in medicine are apparent or not, most medical corporations are already on board.
