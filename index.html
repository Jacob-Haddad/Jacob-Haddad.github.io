Healthcare and Medical Advantages

The application of AI in everyday life is endless, but one of the biggest places people point to when talking about the usefulness of AI is the medical field. Artificial Intelligence could be used to store, organize and retrieve the information on patients and their conditions. In medical diagnosis we can create programs that simulate expert human reasoning. Artificial intelligence could make the work of doctors easier and faster as electronic health records are used. Whether the benefits of AI in medicine are apparent or not, most medical corporations are already on board. Below is a graph that shows an estimated 40% increase in market interest for AI systems in the medical field. This raises the current percent from 40% to 80% interest.

Buisness and the Stock Market

Another field that AI has or can vastly improve is Finance, including algorithmic trading and data mining. Algorithmic trading is the process of using AI systems to make trading decisions at rates much faster than any human could do. In the span of a single minute, an AI system could process the information of over hundreds if not thousands of trades and determine the best outcome. On the other side is data mining, which is the process of extracting data from large sets of patterns. Data mining for business applications can be integrated into a complex modeling and decision-making processes. When it comes to discovering data from patterns and other repetitive processes, AI reigns supreme.

Xiuquan Li, an expert in Artificial intelligence had this to say...

"At the rate that AI is adapting and evolving, you won't need a stock broker anymore because AI will have taken over the market."
