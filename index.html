What is AI?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is defined by Webster as "The capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior". Think of a computer being able to think, act, and make decisions without human interaction. Sounds cool right? Or maybe you're on the other side of the argument; you think AI is dangerous, or might even destroy humaninty! To get a better idea on where you stand, take the survey below.

So where do you stand?

Maybe your results aren't a suprise to you, maybe you knew you would be one way or the other, or maybe you knew you would end up right in the middle Or maybe, you are suprised by your results. No matter where you stand, there are very good points to both sides of this argument. Thats why its so important to look at all sides.
