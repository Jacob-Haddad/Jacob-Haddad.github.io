ABOUT THE TRISTERO

My story with the Tristero is one of confusion and coincidence. I was doing research at my local library for my high school english club. I picked out an estranged book at the end of the section called The Courier's Tragedy. The book was not very long, but it was able to get so much across within the ten short pages it takes up that it was an easy pcik for me. As I read through it and as I became more and more entranced in the twists and turns, the whole thing started to feel weirdly familiar. The depictions of how the people are murdered in this book (which involves their bones being thrown into a lake) is oddly similar to real life attackes on mail riders. This would be no more of a coincidence until I flipped to the last page, and a small piece of paper fell out with a strange symbol written on it.
